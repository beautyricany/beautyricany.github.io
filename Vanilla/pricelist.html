Pricing FAQs

Why are touch-ups necessary?

Everyone heals differently. A touch-up ensures perfect color saturation and allows us to make any adjustments you'd like.

How long do results last?

Eyebrows: 12-18 months, Eyeliner: 2-3 years, Lips: 2-3 years. Longevity depends on skin type, lifestyle, and aftercare.
