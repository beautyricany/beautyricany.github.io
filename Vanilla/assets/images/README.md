# Logo Placeholder

Place your logo image here. The recommended format is PNG with transparency.

**Filename**: `logo.png`

**Recommended dimensions**: 
- Width: 200-400px
- Height: 50-100px (or maintain your logo's aspect ratio)

The logo will be automatically sized to 50px height in the website header.

**Design considerations**:
- Use a transparent background
- Ensure the logo works well on a black background
- Consider the black, red, and white color scheme
